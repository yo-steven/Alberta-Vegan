Community

Events

We're working hard to host community focused events each month to bring people together. These events are open to everyone!

Monthly Potlucks

Enjoy great food and great company at our monthly potlucks! Our potlucks are typically held on the third Sunday of the month and are open to everyone. Entry cost is $5, or $3 with the VVoA member discount. Simply bring a vegan potluck dish to serve at least 6 people (one dish per adult attending), plus your own plate, cutlery, glass and serving utensil.

Monthly Diner's Club

Try out new Veg-Friendly restaurants as well as old favourites in the company of fellow vegans and vegetarians. Check back each month to see where we are headed. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram to stay updated!
