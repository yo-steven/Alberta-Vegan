What is VegFest Edmonton?

VegFest is a one-day vegan festival hosted by Vegans and Vegetarians of Alberta (VVoA), a volunteer-run non-profit organization that promotes a plant-based lifestyle. This unique event features a wide variety of vendors, live entertainment, and community engagement. Find your new favorite vegan restaurant, enjoy a refreshing alcoholic or non-alcoholic beverage, listen to live music, and learn more about the Alberta plant-based community at VegFest Edmonton!
